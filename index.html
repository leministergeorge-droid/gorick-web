Vision And Mission That Drive Excellence

Our Vision

Gorick has a clear vision of becoming the best engineering company in the area of civil, electrical and mechanical within the country.

Our Mission

To create, disseminate, and integrate knowledge of engineering, science, and technology that expands the civil, electrical, and mechanical engineering knowledge base, which in turn enables the betterment of human society.

Our Services

Professional Services Across Major Engineering Fields

We provide a wide range of engineering services to meet your needs.

Electrical Engineering

PLC programming and human machine interface design, pneumatic and hydraulic on-site field wiring checkouts, development of electrical schematic, production and standby support, control panel design, customer training, modular design, field wiring drawings, electrical design through installation, on-site testing and commissioning, and control system design and architecture.

Civil Engineering

Design services for infrastructure projects, roads, railways, water supply, sewerage systems, water treatment plants, hydroelectric power plants, dams, structural design, supervision, inspection, project control, construction services, testing services, maintenance of completed projects, and scientific research in civil engineering.

Mechanical Engineering

Assessing project requirements, measuring the performance of mechanical components, devices, and engines, maintaining and modifying equipment to ensure that it is safe, reliable, and efficient, testing, evaluating, modifying, and retesting products, and providing technical advice.
